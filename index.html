Why should you start Journaling?

Journaling can be a relaxing way to get your thoughts out from your head. It helps you be in tune with your feelings and with your mood. It can be a great stress reliever and make you realize things you didn't even know were there. Whether you use journaling as a form of gratitude, an emotional release, or as a way to plan your goals, you will be sure to find some amazing benefits from this practice.

How to Journal

Dedicate time every morning or night to write about one prompt OR set a timer and every whatever minutes generate a new prompt to write about OR simply write about your day OR maybe whatever is occupying your mind. There really are no rules, you can even decide if you want to physically write with pen and paper or write in a digital format. The possibilities are endless. If you do decide to write about a prompt, don't stress too much about answering the question, simply write whatever comes to mind. Prompts are meant to inspire, they don't require a perfect response.
